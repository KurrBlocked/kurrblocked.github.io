About Me

Hello. I'm Roger, I graduated from the Georgia Institute of Techonology with a Bachelor's in Computational Media, a branch of Computer Science with a focus on the forms of media created with code. I like to make games, and I like to wear a lot of hats. I want to be able to have a piece of me in every aspect of my games and I have a lot of pride for them. I have a lot visions that I can not act on due to my level of skill. I hope to gain as much experience as possible in hopes of making those visions a reality.
